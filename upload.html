Instructions for Dropping a Complaint & Claiming Rewards

  1. Go to the website in the complaints section.
  2. Upload a photograph of the waste in your locality. (Camera and location access must be allowed.)
  3. The complaint reaches our authorities and they will do the necessary work.
  4. Once the area is cleaned, reward points will be credited to your account.
  5. You may then go to the rewards section to claim offers, vouchers, or cashback.
